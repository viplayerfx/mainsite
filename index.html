    The Video Player with Audio FX

    ViPlayer is a modern cross-platform video player with sound effects. Because of the sound effects, users can have a viewing experience similar to that of a movie theater.

    ViPlayer has: Auto Playback Gain Control, Equalizer, Spatial Audio, Stereo Surround Enhance, Reverberation, ViFX Bass, ViFX Clarity, Cure System, ViFX Spectrum Extend, Compressor, and more.

    About ViFX Sound Effects

    Auto Playback Gain Control

    It is used to dynamically control the audio volume. Allow users to hear the details of Movie's audio scene.

    Stereo Surround

    This effect enhances stereo filed surround sound.

    ViFX Bass

    This effect has 3 modes to enhance the movie's sence bass sound, like Explosion scene or Car crash sence.

    ViPX Clarity

    This effect has 3 modes to enhance human voice. It is used for Speech and Conference

    Equalizer

    This effect adjusts the balance between different frequency components of an audio signal.

    Reverberation

    This effect artificially adds sound space infomations

    ViFX Spatial Sound

    The ViFX Spatial EffectCombine 5.1 or 7.1 multi-channel movies into immersive surround sound, allowing users to experience the sound field environment of the movie scenes and fully immerse themselves in the movie.
